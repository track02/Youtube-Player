Note: When configuring your own hotkeys please be aware of possible conflicts with Firefox's default Keyboard Shortcuts along with any Hotkeys used by your other Web Extensions.

The Default hotkeys are as follows.

  • Play/Pause - Left Shift & Enter
  • Seek Forwards - Left Shift & Plus (+)
  • Seek Backwards - Left Shift & Minus (-)
  • Next Video - Left Shift & Greater Than (>)
  • Restart Video - Left Shift & Less Than (<)
